Direct To Garment (DTG)

We wanted a softer feel to our shirts, something more environmentally friendly. After 9 years of utilizing several types of apparel printing methods, we were at a crossroads. Where to go next?

We hit the road and attended conferences across the country. We spent a year investigating different methods and settled on a system that uses biodegradable ink and complies with the strictest industry standards - a water-based DTG printer.
